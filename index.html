About

With FuzeScreen we want to make sharing your media like photos easier. Thus multiple devices have access to a certain gallery.

  • Display mirroring

    With this application you can extend the physical limits of your display by connecting another device with our app.

  • Video Chat

    It is also possible to have a video conference while you showcase the media files you uploaded.

  • Media sharing

    It is possible to share your photos and videos with other people all around the globe.

  • Room management

    With our easy room management you can create a room within seconds and share it with others.
